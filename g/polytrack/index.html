Description

Polytrack 4.0 is an entertaining and engaging game. The goal of the player is to reach the end of the track while getting the best time and reaching every checkpoint. Speed your way through all 10 tracks and get the best time possible! Jump large jumps and drift around corners with your fast car. If you do not want to play any of the 10 large tracks, you can generate or create your own! Polytrack is a fan favorite of every game possible, for a good reason! Good luck getting a fast-time racer! You can choose between the newest, Polytrack 4.0 BETA, 3.0 and the classic 2.0.
